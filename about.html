About Me

My name is David Askew, and I like to build things.

Sometimes I'm hired to build things. I've worked with some world class organizations. Organizations such as Cerner, Adventist Health, Miami Children's Hospital, Catholic Health Initiatives and many others.

Sometimes I build things that need building. I have helped the PeaceCorps build PC Medlink, an app that helps field operatives get supplies to some of the most remote places on earth.

Sometimes I build things because I want them to exist.

I have authored open source documentation. I make it a point to try and share everything I know.

What will I do in the future? Who knows! Contact me if you've got any good ideas!
